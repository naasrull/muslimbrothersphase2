The Malaysia Nursing Council will be hosting our annual nursing conference this year, "Malaysia Nursing Conference 2020" on DECEMBER 4th-6th, 2020 @ Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre.



The online registration system is now open! We cordially invite you to grab this great opportunity to share, to exchange and to learn with colleagues from around the world.



Malaysia Nursing Conference 2020 provide golden opportunities to guide career in nursing, where one can learn new strategies, work with new technologies and gain ideas from experts at the forepart of medical and healthcare. This event also provide networking opportunities to connect with experts, meet and greet sessions and many more to expand their network horizons. This conferece gather thousands of nurses explored the many ways in which nurses work to achieve universal access to health, not only providing health care but also addressing the social determinants of health, such as education, gender equality, poverty, etc.

 

 

Who should participate ?

The annual conference is intended for nurses in the healthcare and medical industry where professionals throughout the Malaysia health ecosystem connect for education, innovation and networking.

 

This Year's Edition:

The theme for our conference is “Leading innovation in nursing” with interesting and latest conference topics that will instantly pique the interests of participants, also for educational research directed towards innovation.

 

Who will attend ?

The conference anticipate more than 20 speakers, which enables participants to learn from prominent industry experts, university professors, clinical researchers and many more invited from all over the world to share their investigation and experiences on all parts of the rapidly and evolving industry of healthcare, also demonstrating latest healthcare techniques.
